⚡ Precision Programming

We use gyroscope-based turning and precise motor control to ensure repeatable, accurate mission runs. Every movement is calculated to millimeter precision.

Our robot features quick-swap attachments designed for specific missions. This modular approach maximizes versatility while maintaining structural integrity.

📊 Strategic Sequencing

We optimize mission order to maximize points while minimizing risk. Our strategy balances high-value missions with consistent, reliable performance.
